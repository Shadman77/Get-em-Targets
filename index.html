Get 'em Targets

Difficulty : Easy

Instruction: The game contains three levels. In each level there will be varying number of targets and the targets will reset their position randomly at set intervals. Hitting any target will also reset the position for all the targets and the player will recieve a score of 10 for Easy, 20 for Medium and 30 for Hard. The targets will reset 100 times. So a player can at most hit a 100 targets. Moving the mouse moves the targets and the left mouse button is for shooting.
